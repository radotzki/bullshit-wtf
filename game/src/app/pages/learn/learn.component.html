During this game you will be shown some remarkably strange facts, however the interesting bits are missing.

Everyone plays with their smartphones and fills in the blanks with the best lies they can come up with, hopefully the kind which will fool their friends.

All the lies will then be displayed including the actual and citated truth. Your goal is to avoid falling for your friends bullshit and select the truth.

If you select the real answer than you are awarded points. You also get points for each friend you fool.


When you ready to start choose the most responsible person in your group and ask him to create a new game.

Everyone else can join by entering the game PIN.

For the best experience we recommend you get your hands on another screen and present the game there.
